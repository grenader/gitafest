GitaFest. Legal Waiver and GUIDELINES/RULES of CONDUCT

Something to consider:

Address of the property:

534 Gita Nagari Road Port Royal, PA 17082

A non-profit organization established in 1974, Gita Nagari is home to the first and only certified slaughter-free dairy farm in North America. The farm is currently operated by a diverse team of volunteers whose vision is to accelerate global transformation through compassionate living. More information can be found here: https://gnecofarm.org/

Property is completely non-smoking

Regarding the cabin: this is by choice of Homeowners. Regarding the entire property: wildfires are a constant threat in our dry Arizona climate. It is simply not an acceptable risk to allow smoking or fires of any kind. Failure to comply with this requirement will result in loss of deposit.

Pets are not permitted

Although we are animal lovers, due to a number of factors we are unable to allow pets to stay with us.

“Good Neighbor” clause

The farm is in a quiet, rural, residential area. The beauty of the location is the peacefulness it provides. For these reasons, we require noise to be kept to a minimum after dark (sound travels) out of respect for our neighbors, and Guests need to understand that a generally-accepted “family-friendly” atmosphere is to be maintained at all times (i.e, no loud parties or yelling/swearing, no nakedness outside the privacy of the cabin or inappropriate personal conduct, etc.). Please respect all members of the farm and Household.

Payment

Your reservation must be paid in full before arriving at the property. If you have any questions or concerns, then please send e-mail to info@gitafest.com.

Falsified Reservations

Any reservation obtained under false pretenses of any kind will be subject to forfeiture of advance payment and any other prior arrangements, and the party will not be permitted to check in.

Safety on the farm

“Guest” understands that the farm is a rural, rustic place. It is a wonderful place to spend time, and kids love the freedom the property allows, but it is not the “city”. There are lots of uneven surfaces, metal, fencing, rocks, dirt and tools of all kinds throughout the property. Trees, shrubs and vegetation of all sorts (including sharp burrs and cactus) may be present. There is no explicit fencing against unwanted critters. Any fences on the property could be rusty, wires could be sharp, and kids can fall out of trees. We will absolutely assist Guests in utilizing whatever resources are available if anything unfortunate were to happen during your stay, however are not liable for any mishaps.

ISKCON GITA NAGARI GUIDELINES and RULES of CONDUCT

ISKCON Gita Nagari is a dhama, a sacred and holy place, and it is therefore imperative that all residents and guests abide by the following rules or code of conduct:

  1.  Animals
    1.  please do not feed any of the animals (there are devotees whose service is to do this)
    2.  please stay clear of the electrified fences
    3. please do not enter beyond the fences into the pastures where the animals graze
    4. please pet the animals carefully and respectfully under farm adult staff supervision only
    5. please do not startle, scare or tease the animals f. while rides are provided, they are at the riders’ own risk
  2.  Buildings and fixed structures
    1.  do not enter buildings, other than the Temple Room, Dining Hall and Rest Rooms, without authorization, unless you are a resident guest
    2.  do not attempt to fix or cause damage to any buildings and fixed structures
    3. do not vandalize any part of the property
  3. Children
    1.  Our children and visiting children play freely on the property. Kindly ensure that your children are supervised at ALL times.
    2.  If you choose not to supervise your children, please arrange that another adult can be available for your children.
    3.  If this is not arranged, then also know that any adult in the community will most likely interact with your children when the need arises. This implies that you are comfortable with your children being assisted by or chastised by any adult.
    4.  We trust that the adults on the property are trustworthy and capable - however we cannot guarantee this as our property is open to the public. Furthermore, there are a multitude of ways to raise and interact with children and may not always align to your preferred approach.
  4. Community
    1.  Gita Nagari is an intentional community and therefore every resident is to endeavor to practice being a community member who contributes to the upliftment of interpersonal relationships
    2.  Guests are requested to respect this and endeavor likewise while on the premises
  5.  Dress Code
    1. All residents and guests are kindly requested to dress respectfully and chastely while on the premises. This includes longer skirts or long tops over pants for the ladies and shirts and pants for men.
  6.  Driving
    1.  Only licensed drivers with insurance may drive on the property
    2.  The speed limit of 10mph must be adhered to
    3.  Beware of crossing peacocks, cats, rabbits, deer, cows, ground hogs, children, pedestrians, etc
    4.  Take bends and turns very gently at reduced speed else you could end up in a ditch.
    5.  Please park in designated areas only. Do not obstruct roads and building and other entrances
  7. Equipment
    1.  Please do not touch any equipment
    2.  Please do not operate any equipment
  8. Garden
    1.  please do not pick any vegetables, fruit or other items from the garden and greenhouse
    2.  please enter only if accompanied by the Head or designate of the garden
  9. Interpersonal interaction
    1. all persons on the property are to conduct themselves in a respectable manner
    2.  due regard and respect is to be offered to all residents and guests c. it is every person’s responsibility on this property to ensure that he or she contributes towards a positive experience for both him/herself and for others
    3.  when conflicts arise, the involved parties are to immediately take the matter to the managers of the festival who will decide the appropriate response
    4.  no discipline, violence, harsh words and like are to be exchanged. Both parties should be fully aware as their conflict escalates and should seek to resolve the matter with the Temple President
    5.  The management will engage other relevant persons in the resolution of the conflict
  10.  Movies
    1. We do not watch television and movies because Gita Nagari is a dhama. An exception can be made in consultation with the Temple President. In that case only P and PG rated movies may be watched.
  11. Neighbors
    1.  please do not enter the property of neighbors be respectful to our neighbors
  12. Plant life
    1. please do not damage or pick any flowers, herbs, vegetables, plants
    2.  please do not damage the trees in any way
    3.  beware of poison ivy – seek information and advice from residents if you cannot identify it
  13. River
    1.  please do not enter or swim in the river
    2.  please do not throw any items into the river
  14. Safety
    1.  please conduct yourself always in a way to protect your own and other’s safety in your services or while you visit here in Gita Nagari
    2.  safety refers to physical and emotional well-being and security
  15. Soliciting a. no soliciting of any kind or collecting of donations (cash or kind) are allowed on the property except if authorized by the Temple President
